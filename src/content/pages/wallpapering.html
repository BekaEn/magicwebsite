WALLPAPERING

Sophisticated Walls Start with Skilled Hands

Wallpaper has the power to redefine a space completely. Whether it's a bold feature wall or a subtle texture that adds depth, the right wallpaper can turn a plain room into a statement. At Magic Painting, our expert team applies wallpaper with precision, care and an eye for detail that ensures a flawless result.

Serving homes and businesses across Fulham, we treat wallpapering as a true craft. From preparation to final finish, we make sure every corner lines up perfectly and every pattern meets with care.

Our Approach

Flawless Application Every Time

We understand that wallpaper requires expert handling. Creases, bubbles or misaligned patterns simply are not acceptable. That is why we take time to prepare each surface properly and install each sheet with steady hands and full focus.

We work with all types of wall coverings including:

  • Vinyl wallpapers are popular for their durability and ease of cleaning, making them perfect for high-traffic areas and family homes. Textured papers add dimension and visual interest, transforming flat walls into tactile surfaces that catch the light in unique ways. Our team has extensive experience with all varieties, ensuring perfect application regardless of the material's specific characteristics.

  • Natural fiber wallcoverings like grasscloth, sisal, and seagrass bring organic warmth and texture to interiors. These specialty materials require particular expertise to install correctly due to their natural variations and specific handling requirements. Our artisans have the specialist knowledge needed to work with these premium materials, creating seamless installations that highlight their natural beauty.

  • Create dramatic impact with custom murals and designer wallpapers that transform an ordinary wall into a stunning focal point. These statement installations require perfect alignment and flawless application to achieve their intended effect. Our team specializes in these high-impact installations, ensuring that complex patterns and large-scale designs are applied with absolute precision.
