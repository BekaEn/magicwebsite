Our Services

At Firstcall Painting

For your convenience, we provide pick-up and delivery, so you don't have to worry about transport. Transform any room in your home with ease—contact us today to learn more about our services.

  • We offer precise colour matching to ensure you get the perfect hue for your project. Whether you're envisioning something bold or more subdued, we can create the exact shade you need. Simply provide a sample, and we’ll handle the rest.

  • Firstcall Painting offers professional spray painting services for furniture and cabinetry, catering to designers, architects, developers, and homeowners alike. Our spray painting services can refresh your kitchen units or furniture, giving them a brand-new look. In addition, our repair services are ideal for restoring damaged items, all while giving you full control over the colour, sheen, and finish. We also offer free local pick-up and delivery, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.

    For those larger projects, we provide on-site spray painting services, ensuring minimal disruption and maximum efficiency. While we specialise in spray painting, we also offer hand painting options for those who prefer a more personal touch. We even stock a range of designer, low-VOC, self-leveling paint for the DIY enthusiast. Get in touch with us today to find out more.

  • At Firstcall Painting, we offer a range of professional restoration services including furniture repairs, French polishing, lacquer finishes, and stain treatments. Whether you're looking to refresh kitchen cabinetry or restore antique furniture, we’ve got the expertise to deliver superior results. We also provide polishing and repair services for doors, panels, and handrails. Contact us for further details on how we can restore and enhance your furniture.

  • At Firstcall Painting, we're passionate about sustainability. Our services focus on restoring, repairing, reusing, and repurposing your existing furniture to help reduce waste and environmental impact. Let us help you refresh what you already have.

  • In our workshop, we offer a wide range of colours to choose from. If you have a specific shade in mind, we also provide custom colour matching to create your perfect hue. Whether you’re dreaming of a vibrant pop of colour or a soft neutral, we can make it happen. Just bring in a sample or share your vision, and we'll take care of the rest.

  • If you're looking for a sustainable and cost-effective way to update your kitchen, kitchen respraying is the perfect solution. Our service can transform your kitchen's look without the waste of a full renovation. Explore our portfolio of successful kitchen resprays to see the remarkable results we've achieved.
