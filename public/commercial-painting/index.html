Our Services

At Firstcall Painting

our experienced professionals are committed to delivering top-quality results. We use premium materials and advanced techniques to ensure a flawless finish, no matter the size or scope of the project.

  • We’ve successfully completed a wide range of commercial projects, from small boutique shops to expansive corporate offices. With a deep understanding of the unique needs of commercial spaces, we work with a variety of materials, including:

    • Acrylic paints
    • Epoxy coatings
    • Polyurethane finishes
    • Specialised coatings for industrial applications

  • We know how important it is to minimise disruption to your business. Our team works swiftly and efficiently, completing your project on time while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

  • Our commercial painting services cover a variety of needs, including:

    Interior painting

    From walls and ceilings to trim and moulding, we provide a smooth, high-quality finish that meets your specific requirements.

    Exterior painting

    We offer durable, weather-resistant coatings to protect your building’s exterior, ensuring it can withstand the elements while looking its best.

    Specialised coatings

    We provide specialised coatings designed for high-traffic areas, industrial environments, and spaces exposed to moisture, ensuring longevity and durability.
